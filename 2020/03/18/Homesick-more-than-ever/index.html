Homesick, more than ever

Posted on 2020-03-18 | Post modified: 2020-03-18 | |

It is the third week now, self-quarantine still continues. But bad news and new policies keep coming everyday.

My mom is getting more anxious for our situation in Sweden, and the relationship between China and Sweden is getting worse just like the situation here. Frankly, personally I do want go back but the chance of getting sick in the airplane is larger than staying here, especially with a child who can obviously not abey strict regulations.

Big data and safety control are fully implemented back there, yesterday some community staff informed my mom to fill in some sort of form to tell us if we decide to go back, what kind of quarantine we need to do. Wanna go back but cannot…

Some pictures took by my Mom from this morning, homesick, very…

PS. Got my masks from China before travel ban, and it took only 3 days, amazing! Ok, now it is time for body exercises again. Almost one hour every, which never happens before, lol.
